E-Commerce B2B Services

We at NAZZ believe and embrace the E-commerce revolution in the region. With the sudden and behemoth boom of E-commerce, we took serious steps to cope with the task and succeeded to work as an essential partner for most of retailers operating online. Understanding the challenges and sometimes the obstacles of cross-border business, we developed end-to-end solutions for all international inbound to the middle east. We strategically established hubs in United Arab Emirates and in Saudi Arabia to cater for clearance and delivery as well as, our agents are located conveniently all over the world specially from China, USA an Europe.

We will provide the following solutions for you :

  • Back to back shipping where we arrange the shipment from your doorstep to the destination warehouse. We will act as EOR and IOR (Exporters & Importers of Records) which will ensure seamless operations and you will have complete peace of mind. We will process all export documents on your behalf and take full responsibility and risk of the cargo. We will pay customs duty and VAT if applicable and finally, provide receiving notes.
  • Clearance activities: we can act as your clearing agents in the region and you may have the option to be your own EOR & IOR. This way you can control the first mile of your shipments but at the same time, you can ensure error-free clearance and delivery activities.

For more information and details please contact our customer service. We can tailor specific services for your business. All in all, we adapted to all E-commerce needs in the Middle East where you can utilize our expertise to clear all categories and all types of cargo.
